\### Tiara's Workstream (6 pts) - Brand Strategy \& Copy Direction

\- \[ ] Component patterns identified (buttons, cards, forms, navigation, CTAs)

\*\*Copywriting Strategy \& Real-Time Guidance (2 pts):\*\*

\- \[ ] Mona briefed on copy strategy before she starts prompting

\- \[ ] Mona's Relume prompts guided with specific messaging in real-time

\- \[ ] Relume-generated copy reviewed and refined (adjust prompts if too generic)

\- \[ ] Final wireframe copy approved by end of week

\*\*Sitemap Structure Setup in Relume (1 pt):\*\*

\- \[ ] Responsive breakpoints configured (Desktop: 1440px, Tablet: 768px, Mobile: 375px)

\- \[ ] Discovery sitemap referenced for structure



\*\*Generate All Pages with Strategic Copy (2 pts):\*\*
