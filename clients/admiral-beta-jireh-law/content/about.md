- Firm Description

**Boutique litigation firm** built for efficiency, staffed by experienced trial lawyers. We deliver pragmatic, results-driven litigation and responsive, cost-efficient advocacy for businesses and individuals.

Associates:

*Wei Ern graduated from the National University of Singapore with an LL.B. (Second Class (Upper Division) Honours) and was called to the Singapore Bar in 2024\.*

*Prior to joining Jireh Law LLC, Wei Ern completed his practice training and began his legal career at a boutique law firm, where he was part of the Civil Litigation and Family & Matrimonial teams. He has assisted in matters before both the State Courts and High Court of Singapore, and has experience advising and representing clients in a wide range of civil and commercial disputes.*

*Above all, Wei Ern views his legal practice as a form of service and strives to guide his clients through the complexities of the legal process with empathy and diligence.*

*Outside of work, Wei Ern is an avid sports enthusiast who enjoys staying active and can often be found on the football pitch. He also remains actively involved in his local church community.*
