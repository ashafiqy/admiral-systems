Joshua is a disputes-focused lawyer with substantial experience in the following areas of contentious work: 

**Litigation**

* Primarily specialize in complex commercial disputes, regularly appearing in the Supreme Court and State Courts of Singapore.  
* Regularly manage matters end-to-end, including pre-trial strategy, discovery applications, urgent injunctions, and full trials.  
* Expertise in various areas including:  
    
  * Complex contractual disputes  
  * Employment disputes, especially in relation to disputes over compensation concerning high ranking corporate executives  
  * Fraud and trust-related disputes  
  * Asset Recovery  
  * Joint venture and shareholder disputes  
  * Breach of directors’ duties  
  * Minority oppression  
  * Defamation  
  * MCST (Management Corporation Strata Title) matters  
  * Construction-related disputes  
  * Land disputes

**Arbitration**

* Experience in Singapore International Arbitration Centre (SIAC) arbitrations, handling complex commercial disputes through arbitration processes.  
    
* Key cases include:  
  * Cross-border arbitration involving the attempted entry of pharmaceutical products into China, addressing intricate regulatory and contractual issues.  
  * Cross-border arbitration involving a Malaysian-based company providing irrigation services in Singapore, focusing a joint venture obligations and deadlock issues. 

**Criminal Defence**

* Represent accused persons in a variety of criminal charges, including:  
  * Financial and regulatory crimes  
  * Sexual crimes  
  * Violence-related crimes  
* Substantial trial experience as a defence lawyer, notably having completed a **45-day criminal trial**.  
* Conducted numerous Magistrate's Appeals in the High Court.  
* Experienced also internal regulatory investigations, preparing reports for board members.
