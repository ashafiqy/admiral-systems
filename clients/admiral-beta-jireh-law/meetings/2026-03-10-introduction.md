### Service Areas and Complexity Breakdown

- Focus on these 4 main services in priority order:
  1. Litigation (commercial and civil)
  2. Arbitration
  3. Criminal defense
  4. Internal regulatory investigations and crisis management
- Commercial litigation: When businesses fight
  - Shareholder disputes, breach of joint venture agreements, director duties breaches, minority oppression claims
- Civil litigation: Personal disputes over money
  - House ownership, landlord-tenant disputes, will distribution issues
  - Divorce falls under separate family/matrimonial litigation (not Joshua’s focus)
- Complexity rules of thumb:
  - Below $200K typically non-complex
  - Commercial disputes involving businesses tend to be more complex
  - Rich individuals fighting (especially family + business combinations) can be worst cases
- Healthy mix preferred: 3-4 complex cases per year plus simpler cases to pay bills
