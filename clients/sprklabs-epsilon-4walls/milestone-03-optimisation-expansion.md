# Milestone 03 — Optimisation & Expansion
**4Walls Growth Partnership**

**Supplier:** Sprklabs — Valentin Pietra
**Client:** 4 Walls Agents Ltd — Oliver Thomas Lawson Bates
**Phase Duration:** 23 Jun – 22 Aug 2026 (Months 5–6)
**Phase Status:** Pending — begins after Milestone 02 sign-off

---

## Objective

With the core automation stack live, this phase shifts focus to performance. Every sequence, flow, and touchpoint built in Milestone 02 gets interrogated against real data — what's converting, what's stalling, and what needs to change. Alongside optimisation, this phase opens up AEO/SEO as a secondary acquisition channel and establishes the monthly reporting rhythm that will carry through to the end of the engagement.

> This phase does not begin until Milestone 02 deliverables are accepted and all automations are confirmed live.

---

## What Gets Done

### Continuous A/B Testing & Optimisation (Ongoing / 23 Jun – 22 Aug 2026)

**Sprklabs actions:**
- Analyse A/B test results from Milestone 02 and apply winning variants across sequences
- Run new experiments across emails (subject lines, body copy, CTAs, send timing) and landing pages
- Iterate on ManyChat flows based on booking drop-off data
- Refine buyer segment messaging based on engagement and conversion data
- Optimise cross-sell sequences using viewing history and CRM data

**4Walls actions:**
- Implement any landing page or form changes recommended by Sprklabs
- Maintain updated lead status and attribution data in CRM throughout

---

### Performance Refinement (23 Jun – 22 Jul 2026)

**Sprklabs actions:**
- Full review of all active sequences against baseline metrics from Milestone 01
- Identify underperforming segments or touchpoints
- Adjust nurture cadence and messaging for "not yet" buyers (6-month drip)
- Review ContentSquare heatmaps and session data; refine UX recommendations

**4Walls actions:**
- Provide monthly sales activity summary for leads generated by automations
- Implement any UX changes from updated Figma mockups

---

### AEO/SEO Exploration (Secondary Channel) (23 Jul – 22 Aug 2026)

**Sprklabs actions:**
- Audit current SEO standing via GA4 + Google Search Console
- Identify AEO/SEO quick wins and longer-term content opportunities
- Deliver a content roadmap with prioritised opportunities
- Define keyword and topic clusters relevant to 4Walls' buyer audience

> AEO/SEO is a secondary focus. The primary KPIs remain reducing manual booking ratio and increasing automated viewings.

**4Walls actions:**
- Review and feedback on content roadmap within 5 business days of delivery
- Implement any on-page SEO recommendations (meta tags, page structure) as specified

---

### Monthly Reporting & KPI Reviews

**Sprklabs delivers monthly:**
- Attribution report: which automations are driving bookings
- KPI performance vs. targets (Tier 1, 2, and 3 bonus thresholds)
- A/B test results summary
- Recommended actions for the following month

| Report | Frequency | Delivery Format |

| A/B Test Results | Monthly | Included in attribution report |
| AEO/SEO Opportunity Report | Once (Aug 2026) | Document |
| Content Roadmap | Once (Aug 2026) | Document |
