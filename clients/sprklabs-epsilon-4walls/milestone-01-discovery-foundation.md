# Milestone 01 — Discovery & Foundation
**4Walls Growth Partnership**

**Supplier:** Sprklabs — Valentin Pietra
**Client:** 4 Walls Agents Ltd — Oliver Thomas Lawson Bates
**Phase Duration:** 23 Feb – 22 Mar 2026 (4 weeks)
**Phase Status:** In Progress

---

## Objective

Establish a clear baseline of 4Walls' current performance, gain full access to all required platforms, audit existing flows and designs, and define the strategic foundation for everything that follows. No automation is built in this phase — the goal is to understand what exists, what's broken, and where the fastest wins are.

---

## Week-by-Week Breakdown

### Week 1–2: Access & Audit
**23 Feb – 8 Mar 2026**

**Sprklabs actions:**
- Audit existing email sequences and WhatsApp flows
- Audit existing design and planned improvements (landing pages, forms, booking flow, Figma designs for property search, questionnaires, case studies)
- Document baseline metrics across all key indicators

**Baseline metrics to capture:**
| Metric | Baseline Target to Document |
|---|---|
| Lead → Viewing Conversion Rate | Current % (target: understand gap to 30%) |
| Manual Booking Ratio | Current % (known: ~50%) |
| Monthly Viewing Volume | Current number |
| Email Open Rates | Current % |

**4Walls must provide by 28 Feb (within 5 business days of start):**
- Admin access to: Loops, ManyChat, GA4, Google Search Console, ContentSquare
- View access to: Custom CRM, Figma design files

> If access is not provided by 28 Feb, Sprklabs may invoice the setup fee but will delay work commencement. Timeline milestones will shift accordingly.

---

### Week 3–4: Strategy & Quick Wins
**9 Mar – 22 Mar 2026**

**Sprklabs actions:**
- Define 3 buyer segments (by intent, timeline, and budget)
- Design attribution taxonomy for CRM
- Implement quick wins: deliverability fixes + subject line testing framework
- Set up ContentSquare tracking

**4Walls actions:**
- Review and sign off on buyer segment definitions
- Implement attribution taxonomy in custom CRM (Sprklabs specifies; 4Walls builds)
- Add ContentSquare tracking snippets to relevant pages
