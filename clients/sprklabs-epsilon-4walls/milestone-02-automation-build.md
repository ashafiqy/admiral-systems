# Milestone 02 — Automation Build
**4Walls Growth Partnership**

**Supplier:** Sprklabs — Valentin Pietra
**Client:** 4 Walls Agents Ltd — Oliver Thomas Lawson Bates
**Phase Duration:** 23 Mar – 22 Jun 2026 (12 weeks / Weeks 5–16)
**Phase Status:** Pending — begins after Milestone 01 sign-off

---

## Objective

Build and launch the full automation stack that converts leads into booked viewings without manual intervention. This phase delivers the core of the engagement: segmented email sequences, optimised WhatsApp booking flows, speed-to-lead automation, cross-sell engine, and a live A/B testing framework — all wired together with proper CRM attribution.

> This phase does not begin until Milestone 01 deliverables are accepted and all baseline data is confirmed.

---

## What Gets Built

### Email & WhatsApp Optimisation (Weeks 5–10 / 23 Mar – 3 May 2026)

**Sprklabs builds:**
- 3 segmented email sequences in Loops:
  - **Welcome sequence** — for new leads entering the funnel
  - **Nurture sequence** — for leads actively evaluating
  - **Re-engagement sequence** — for leads that have gone cold
- Optimised ManyChat booking flows — reduce friction, guide leads to self-book
- Speed-to-lead automation — instant response triggered the moment a lead comes in, followed by structured follow-up sequences
- A/B testing framework — subject lines, CTAs, send times; structured experiments with clear success metrics

**4Walls delivers:**
- Figma mockup implementation (code changes to landing pages and forms)
- ID tags and GTM snippets added to pages as specified by Sprklabs
- Any CRM trigger connections required for automation to fire correctly

---

### Cross-Sell Engine (Weeks 11–14 / 4 May – 31 May 2026)

**Sprklabs builds:**
- Cross-sell logic design: "viewed X, consider Y" recommendation framework
- Cross-sell sequences in Loops + ManyChat — automated flows that suggest relevant properties to leads who have already booked a viewing
- Coordination with 4Walls on CRM triggers needed to power the recommendation engine

**4Walls delivers:**
- CRM trigger implementation to feed property data into cross-sell logic
- Review and approval of cross-sell logic before sequences go live

---

### Test, Refine & ContentSquare Review (Weeks 15–16 / 1 Jun – 22 Jun 2026)

**Sprklabs actions:**
- Full end-to-end testing of all sequences and flows
- Refinement based on initial live data
- ContentSquare data review — identify design improvement opportunities for next phase
- Provide Figma mockups for priority UX improvements (4Walls implements)

**4Walls actions:**
- UAT (User Acceptance Testing) across all automations
- Sign-off on all live sequences before phase close
