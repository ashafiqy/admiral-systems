# 4Walls Growth Partnership — Project Brief

**Client:** 4 Walls Agents Ltd — Oliver Thomas Lawson Bates
**Supplier:** Valentin Pietra — Sprklabs
**Document Date:** 11 Feb 2026 · Version 1.0
**Engagement Start:** 23 Feb 2026
> Note: This is partnership pricing for a referred client relationship.

---

## 1. Problem Statement

4Walls has built a sophisticated custom tech stack (proprietary CRM, CMS, and booking system) that handles lead capture and property listings. The missing piece is an **automation layer** that converts those leads into booked viewings at scale without manual intervention.

| Problem | Pain Level | Current Impact |
|---|---|---|
| 50% of bookings require manual calls | **Critical** | You're the bottleneck; can't scale |
| No cross-sell automation | **Critical** | Leaving viewings (and sales) on the table |
| Email open rates are low | **High** | Missing warm leads who go cold |
| No segmented buyer communication | **High** | One-size-fits-all messaging underperforms |
| Cashflow timing concerns | **High** | Hard to invest ahead of revenue |
| No property search/filters on site | **Medium** | Buyers bounce; competitors capture them |
| CRM attribution unclear | **Medium** | Can't tell what's working |

---

## 2. Engagement Focus

1. **Lifecycle Marketing Optimization** — Improving email (Loops) and WhatsApp (ManyChat) sequences to increase conversion without manual calls
2. **Cross-Sell Automation** — Building "you viewed X, consider Y" flows to maximise viewings per lead
3. **Analytics & Attribution** — Setting up proper tracking to measure what's working
4. **Design Advisory** — Providing UX recommendations that 4Walls' team implements

> Sprklabs will coordinate with 4Walls on any CMS/CRM technical implementation.

## 9. Acceptance & Testing

### Acceptance Process

1. Sprklabs presents the deliverable to the client
2. Client provides review and feedback within the agreed timeline
3. If no feedback is provided within the agreed timeline, deliverable is **automatically accepted**
4. If feedback is provided, Sprklabs addresses it within the scope of the SOW
5. Once feedback is addressed, client has **1 business day** for final acceptance

### Testing Methodology

- **Development Testing** — Sprklabs performs internal testing before submission
- **User Acceptance Testing (UAT)** — Client tests implementation against defined requirements
- **Performance Testing** — Speed, load times, and mobile responsiveness tested against industry benchmarks
- **Integration Testing** — All system integrations (email marketing, analytics, etc.) tested for proper data flow

## 10. Assumptions & Exclusions

### Key Assumptions

1. **Dedicated Point of Contact** — Client assigns a named decision-maker to collaborate with Sprklabs throughout
2. **Timely Feedback** — Delays in review or milestone acceptance may extend delivery timelines proportionally
3. **Meeting Availability** — Client team available for regular strategy and ad-hoc meetings
4. **Platform Access** — 4Walls provides access to Loops, ManyChat, GA4, GSC, ContentSquare, CRM (view), and Figma within **5 business days of contract signature**. If not provided, Sprklabs may invoice the setup fee but delay work start; timelines shift accordingly
5. **Media & Brand Assets** — Client provides all required assets in a timely manner; stock/paid imagery not included
6. **Copy** — Sprklabs provides one round of copy (draft); client reviews and amends
7. **Third-Party Costs** — All third-party tool fees paid directly by the client
8. **Digital Advertising** — Ad spend managed and paid by the client; not in scope
9. **Mutual Transparency (Performance Model)**
   - Sprklabs provides: real-time dashboard/data access, weekly lead status reports, full campaign metrics
   - 4Walls provides: CRM access showing lead progression, feedback on lead quality, monthly sales activity summary
   - Shared responsibility for maintaining updated lead status and attribution tracking
10. **Code Warranty** — 90-day warranty from go-live on Sprklabs-authored code; excludes third-party code or client modifications
11. **Technical Implementation** — All code changes, landing page updates, and CRM modifications implemented by 4Walls' team. Sprklabs specifies; 4Walls builds. Delays in 4Walls implementation proportionally extend Sprklabs deliverable timelines
12. **Custom CRM Integration** — Attribution tracking designed by Sprklabs, implemented by 4Walls; tagging taxonomy agreed before implementation
13. **AEO/SEO is Secondary** — Included but not the primary focus; primary KPI is reducing manual bookings and increasing automated viewings

### Out of Scope

- Any development work (4Walls team handles all code)
- CRM development or modification
- Photography or video production
- Paid advertising management
- Social media management
- Translation services
- Legal or financial consulting
- Sales calls and closing activities (remain with the client)
- Custom integrations beyond standard tools
