# Milestone 04 — Scale & Maintain
**4Walls Growth Partnership**

**Supplier:** Sprklabs — Valentin Pietra
**Client:** 4 Walls Agents Ltd — Oliver Thomas Lawson Bates
**Phase Duration:** 23 Aug 2026 – 22 Feb 2027 (Months 7–13)
**Phase Status:** Pending — begins after Milestone 03 sign-off

---

## Objective

The full automation stack is live, tested, and optimised. This phase is about running it at scale — sustaining performance, keeping automations current as new developments come to market, expanding what's working, and preparing the system to handle increasing lead volume as 4Walls grows. Monthly reporting and KPI reconciliation continue throughout.

> This is the steady-state phase. The focus shifts from building to operating, scaling, and continuously improving.

---

## What Gets Done

### Steady-State Optimisation (Ongoing / Aug 2026 – Feb 2027)

**Sprklabs actions:**
- Monitor all live sequences (Loops + ManyChat) for performance degradation or deliverability issues
- Run continuous A/B experiments — rotate winning variants, test new hypotheses
- Adjust segmentation and messaging as buyer behaviour data accumulates
- Refine cross-sell logic as new property data and viewing history builds up
- Monthly attribution reporting: what's driving bookings, what's not
- Quarterly strategy review: assess overall system performance and prioritise next improvements

---

### Automation Updates for New Developments (Rolling)

As 4Walls brings new properties or developments to market, existing automations need to reflect them.

**Sprklabs actions:**
- Update cross-sell sequences to include new developments
- Adjust nurture and re-engagement messaging to reference current inventory
- Brief 4Walls on any CRM tagging updates needed for new properties

**4Walls actions:**
- Notify Sprklabs of new developments ahead of launch (minimum 2 weeks notice recommended)
- Implement any CRM tagging updates specified by Sprklabs
- Implement any landing page or form updates from Figma mockups

---

### Scale What's Working (Ongoing)

**Sprklabs actions:**
- Identify highest-performing sequences and expand their reach (new segments, new channels)
- Explore additional ManyChat channels (Instagram DM, Facebook Messenger) if WhatsApp performance warrants it
- Increase cadence or volume on sequences that are converting above target
- Continue AEO/SEO content execution against the roadmap delivered in Milestone 03

**4Walls actions:**
- Implement any additional platform integrations required to expand reach
- Review and approve expanded sequence logic before go-live

---

### Prepare for Increased Lead Volume (Q4 2026 – Q1 2027)

**Sprklabs actions:**
- Stress-test automation sequences against higher lead volume scenarios
- Identify any bottlenecks in the booking flow that may emerge at scale
- Review Loops and ManyChat plan tiers — flag if subscriber/contact limits require upgrades
- Deliver scaling recommendations report by Jan 2027

**4Walls actions:**
- Upgrade third-party tool plans as required (Loops, ManyChat, ContentSquare)
- Ensure CRM is capable of handling increased data volume

---

### Monthly Reporting & KPI Reconciliation (Every Month)

**Sprklabs delivers:**
- Attribution report: which automations and channels are driving bookings
- KPI performance vs. Tier 1, 2, and 3 bonus thresholds
- A/B test results and applied changes
- Recommended priorities for the following month

| Report | Frequency | Delivery Format |
|---|---|---|

| A/B Test Results | Monthly | Included in attribution report |
| Scaling Recommendations Report | Once (Jan 2027) | Document |
| End-of-Term Review | Once (Feb 2027) | Document + virtual meeting |
