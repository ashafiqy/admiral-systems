# AI Summary

### Website Improvements Discussion

- Designer went too deep into revamping entire website
- Focus areas identified:
  - Hero section on homepage needs clarity improvement
  - Property listing page requires enhancement
  - Form creation for lead capture when primary property doesn’t match user needs
- Current issue: Users landing from Meta ads on wrong property type have no way to express actual preferences
  - Missing opportunity to capture lead data for follow-up
  - No mechanism to redirect to relevant properties

### AI Positioning & Messaging

- Current “AI powered platform” tagline needs evolution
- Reality: AI is Meta’s targeting system, not proprietary tech
  - 4walls optimizes Meta’s AI for property matching
  - Future H2 development will include actual AI personas for property recommendations
- Agreed to maintain AI messaging without specifying implementation details
- Will become genuinely AI-focused by H2 with persona-based matching

### Lead Capture & Email Strategy

- Current workflow issues identified:
  - Simultaneous email + WhatsApp creates overwhelming user experience
  - Split attention across channels reduces effectiveness
- Proposed improvements:
  1. Merge confirmation email with brochure/documentation
  2. Delay WhatsApp by 5-10 minutes for human-like timing
  3. Include booking link in communications
- Segmentation discussion: investor vs first-time buyer
  - Investors focus on yield potential
  - First-time buyers need process reassurance
  - Currently only using sales inquiries (rent inquiries rejected due to Meta algorithm issues)

### Technical Infrastructure Updates

- Calendar system: Custom-built, live and functional
  - Links tied to individual customers with development switching capability
  - Unified availability management across viewing agents
- Data progress: Scraped all London development data
  - 8 of top 10 developers coming onboard
  - Close to full property listing capability
- Integration challenge: Consolidating email (Loops - $50/month) and WhatsApp (ManyChat - $25/month) workflows
  - Target: Single workflow under $100/month budget

### Next Steps

- Designer to focus on homepage hero and listing page only
- Valentin to research unified email/WhatsApp platform options
- Oli to send booking system links for email campaign integration
- James to add CRM buttons for workflow improvement
- Maintain focus on 48-hour lead conversion window
